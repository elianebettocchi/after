Further

You try to move to follow the person, but your legs don't work, they bend in the most weird directions! While trying to control them, you end up kicking the ground and your body floats in a random direction. You then realize that you can move forward using these small kicks against any hard surface. However, every time you kick something, you feel drained. Kicking and bumping you finally reach the person and go through them again.
Surprise, you feel your energy restored! Then you notice something that resembles a flower in the person's nape; it glows and pulsates, as if calling you. Instinctively you reach for this flower and, voilá, you attach your hand to the person's shoulder. As soon as this happen, you begin to listen to the person's thoughts and, even better, you feel what they feel!!!
