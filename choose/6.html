Further

you try to move to explore the place, but your legs don't work, they bend in the most weird directions! While trying to control them, you end up kicking the ground and your body floats in a random direction. You then realize that you can move forward using these small kicks against any hard surface. However, every time you kick something, you feel drained.
Kicking and bumping, you finally reach a source of water and desperately start to drink, but it doesn't satisfy you enough. Actually, the more you drink, the more thirsty you feel. You remember how energized you felt after passing through that person. There's no one around and you feel terribly scared to leave this place. The best course of action seems to be wait for someone to show up.
