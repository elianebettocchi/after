Further

[2] The darkness is dissipating. You blink and open your eyes. You see and feel the same material around you. You try to move, but you are in the same situation you were before. But now everything is silent and motionless. You have no idea how long you've been like that, but you do start to feel itchy. You try to reach the itchy spots but you are totally paralyzed.
The itchy spots spread throughout your body and become gradually horribly painful until it becomes numb. At the same time, you experience the most foul smell around you, and soon you have the weird sensation as if you are melting, becoming shapeless, light, ethereal...
You try to move and finally you are free. You burst through whatever was surrounding you and you see a familiar place. You see a person coming to you, looking at you but the gaze seems to aim beyond you! Before you can move, the person walks through you and for a brief moment you can see the insides of the body, which makes you feel sick, but at the same time, full of energy!!!

What do you do:
Follow the person
